## Plugins
- [maximise-active-pane-obsidian: A plugin for [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) which simply fills the workspace with the active pane.](https://github.com/deathau/maximise-active-pane-obsidian)
- [obsidian-codemirror-options: Obsidian plugin with options to customize the behavior of CodeMirror](https://github.com/nothingislost/obsidian-codemirror-options)
- [obsidian-copy-as-latex: Quick plugin to be able to copy/paste from Obsidian/Markdown into a Latex document](https://github.com/mo-seph/obsidian-copy-as-latex)
- [pane-relief: Obsidian plugin for per-pane history, pane movement/navigation hotkeys, and more](https://github.com/pjeby/pane-relief)
